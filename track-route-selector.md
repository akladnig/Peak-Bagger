# Multiple track/route popup
Adds a popup to allow selection of track/route, when a click hits multiple tracks or routes.

- Surfaces a popup similar to tr-select.png
- The popup displays a list of the tracks and routes, allowing selection of the track or route
- On selection close the popup and surface the info panel
- The popup displays a list of tiles with the following details show for each tile:
	-  a micro-map of the track or route on the LHS.
	- The track or route name in bold to the right of the micro-map
	- A second line of text under the track or route name showing "Track" or "Route", distance, time, date
