# My Year to Date Card
## Goal
Add a 7th card to show key statistics for the Year to Date

## Features
Card Title: "My Year to Date"
- Displays the following statistics for the year to date:
  - Distance
  - Ascent
  - Number of Walks
  - Peaks climbed

- To be rendered as:
  - Kilometers walked:   distance
  - Metres climbed:      elevation
  - Total Walks:         count of walks
  - Peaks Climbed:       count of total peaks climbed
  - New Peaks Climbed:   count of new peaks

The Prev/Next buttons should move to the previous/next year. The Card title should probably be changed to match the year - "My Walks in yyyy"
