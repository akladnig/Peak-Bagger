# Add a AppBar to the application
- Add a common AppBar to the top of the app which is used by every screen
- occupies the full width of the screen
- the app icon will need to moved from the left hand menu to the top left of the AppBar
- The AppBar will contain a title to the left of the bar, spaced away from the app icon. 
- the top of the left hand menu will start from the bottom of the AppBar.
- the AppBar will have a drop shadow at the bottom
- the theme icon will need to be relocated to the top right of the AppBar 
- replace the current headings in Settings and ObjectBox Admin with the new AppBar
