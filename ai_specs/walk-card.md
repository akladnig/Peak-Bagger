# Latest Walk Card
## Goal
Create the content for the Latest Walk Card

## Content
- The Top Row of the card should be the Track Name styled as bold text
- The next row should display on a single row:
  - date e.g. Thu 14 May 2026
  - distance e.g. 10.5 km
  - ascent e.g. 638 m  
- At the bottom display the newest gpx track from GpxTrack in a mini-map, as a 4:3 aspect ratio.
